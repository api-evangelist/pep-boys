# Pep Boys (pep-boys)
Pep Boys is an American automotive aftermarket service chain founded in 1921 and headquartered in Bala Cynwyd, Pennsylvania. Owned by Icahn Enterprises since 2016, Pep Boys completed its transition away from retail parts in December 2023 and now operates as a service-only chain with roughly 850 to 900+ service centers across the U.S. and Puerto Rico, including a Pep Boys Mobile Crew offering for fleet and on-location maintenance. Pep Boys does not publish a public developer program, OpenAPI definition, SDKs, or webhooks.
